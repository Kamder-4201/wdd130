History

Founded in 1995, our company has guided thousands of adventurers down rivers across the country. What started as a small group of enthusiasts has grown into a community that shares a passion for nature, excitement, and friendship.

Over the years, we've expanded our trips, trained expert guides, and introduced sustainable practices to protect the rivers we love. Our mission remains the same: creating unforgettable experiences for every adventurer.
